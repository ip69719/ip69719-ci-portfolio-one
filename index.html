Our Services

Assessment



We conduct thorough pediatric Occupational Therapy assessments to identify each child’s unique needs.


A child is assessed using standardised testing, clinical observations and questionnaires to investigate his/her general and fine motor coordination, visual perception and sensory processing.


Our comprehensive reports includes assessment results and recommendations.


Please allow two hours for this appointment.

Intervention



We will develop individual Therapy Programs following the assessment. These programmes are designed to meet the individual needs of each child in order to maximise his/her development.


We encourage active involvement from parents to ensure that skills learnt in the therapy sessions can be practised at home.


Therapy sessions are typically 45 minutes long.

School Visits



Poor learning environment can impact how your child learns and behaves at school.


We work together with teachers in order to improve your child’s learning experience.


Our Occupational Therapists can provide teaching staff with recommendations on how to best support your child in school environment.
